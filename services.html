In-Depth Care

Department Highlights

Emergency & Trauma Care

Our trauma center is fully equipped to handle all medical and surgical emergencies. With a dedicated 24/7 team of emergency physicians, surgeons, and critical care specialists, we ensure that patients receive immediate life-saving treatments. Our advanced Burn Unit and fully AC ICU wards provide a sterile and monitored environment for optimal recovery.

Advanced Surgical Procedures

Featuring modular operation theaters with modern anesthesia workstations, we perform a wide spectrum of surgeries. From minimally invasive laparoscopy to complex neuro and spinal surgeries, our experienced surgeons prioritize patient safety and quick recovery times.

Comprehensive Diagnostics

Accurate diagnosis is the cornerstone of effective treatment. Our in-house pathology lab, ECG, ECHO, and advanced imaging facilities provide rapid and reliable results, enabling our doctors to formulate precise treatment plans without delay.
